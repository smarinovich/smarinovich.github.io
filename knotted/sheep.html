SHEEPSHANK


A Sheepshank is used to shorten and strengthen a length of rope. By giving the rope a mechincal advantage of 3:1, this will allow it hold more weight, and can shrink a rope which may be too long, and in the way.

To tie a Sheepshank you will need:
- A middle section of rope
