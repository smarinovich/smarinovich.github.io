ALPINE BUTTERFLY


An Alpine Butterfly is a knot used to create a loop within the middle of a rope. This is useful to connect a carabiner to the rope, or can be used to create footholds for a ladder (if the holes are big enough).

To tie an Alpine Butterfly you will need:
- A middle section of rope
