ROLLING HITCH


A Rolling Hitch is a knot where the rope is tied on top of itself. This is useful to tie out guy ropes, and secure it to tent pegs, so a tent fly is streched properly and does not fly away.

To tie a Rolling Hitch you will need:
- A single end of rope
- A log, or tent peg, or something to secure the knot to
