CLOVE HITCH


A Clove Hitch is used to tie a piece of rope to a log or pole. This is useful for starting off a lashing, or a bigger knot which can conneect multiple poles together, or to make a fence, and connect a rope ot each of the fence poles.

To tie a Clove Hitch you will need:
- A single end of rope
- A Pole or Log (something to tie the hitch to)
